A Little About Me

Most notably I really really loath fixing user email problems!

I have an immense passion for everything film and theater! I have taken some acting (for the stage and in front of camera) and directing classes, which led to me being in a few student films as well as getting involved with local film competitions such as the Seattle 48hr Film Fest. Which if you have never participated in I HIGHLY encourage it!
